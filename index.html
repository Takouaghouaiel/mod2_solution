Our Menu

Chicken

The restaurant “Le Méditerranée” has an exceptional location in the marina and welcomes you in a unique setting with dreamlike colours of white and turquoise blue. The view flies towards a horizon lost off the coast and then come back to the many boats that have chosen to anchor in this little corner of paradise. This is only the beginning, and lunch or dinner which give you the choice between European and Tunisian cuisine quickly starts with a friendly and professional service. The specialties are varied: meat, poultry, fish, lobster ... You just need to choose, to taste and relax. The restaurant seats up to 250 people.
